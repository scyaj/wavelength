Adopt a Companions


Isn't it weird for a lifestyle store to also be an animal shelter? Well, we're not one exactly. We partner with Pronto Pets to bring more awareness to the plethora of pets that are always waiting to be adopted. While we just spread the word, the official adoption process is through Pronto Pets.


Companions Available

Perry and Patty the Parakeets

Perry and Patty are a bonded pair, and must go together! While they have a lifelong bond, that doesn't mean they can't both make room for you as well.

Both are trained as messenger birds, and will learn very quickly where their new 'home location' is.


Meza the Mongrel

Don’t let the name fool you! Meza may not be a purebred, but that doesn’t stop him from being a well-trained, and loyal Companion.

He loves treats, and spending time outdoors.


Supra the Cat

Though just a little kitten, Supra is an intelligent cat that requires the same sharpness of her owners.

Her favorite things in the world are bird watching, and climbing
