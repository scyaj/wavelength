Wavelength Wares' Story

Wavelength Wares, and this website are inspired by retro computers, aesthetics of the 80s/90s, and cyberpunk in general. This project ombines the coding knowledge I've gained over this semester, as well as being a practice in creativity, and world building.

Style Guide

This fictional universe was made with the following themes in mind:

Ethereal

While Etherealism isn't a real movement (to my knowledge), I've always thought it a beautiful word. Also a juxtaposition to the usually dark, and devilish aesthetic of cyberpunk.

Retro-Futurism

Chunky buttons, tactile feedback, neon strips. A mix of old, and new technology.

E-Commerce

Though outside the scope of the Coding class, I wanted to challenge myself, and not "get too comfortable" with variety of websites already taught.
